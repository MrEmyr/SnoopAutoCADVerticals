## Archive Policy

Documents are archived when:
1. They describe completed work that is no longer being developed
2. They have been superseded by newer, more accurate documentation
3. They represent historical snapshots of development phases
4. They are no longer referenced by active documentation

Documents are **NOT** archived if:
1. They are plans or drawings (`.drawio` files)
2. They contain current technical specifications
3. They are actively maintained user guides
4. They describe ongoing or future work
